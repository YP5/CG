Functions:

1. Ambient, diffuse and specular parameters could be changed by sliders. Specular and diffuse are complementary.

2. 3 light sources are available, one free to be changed. Locations are modified based on which object is being viewed. 
   They can be turned on and off.

3. The dophins are available to be viewed. since material properties are definied, those options are not available to change.

4. 9 viewing positions are provided to check out the view. One is from the front, 8 are from each corner of the viewing volume.
